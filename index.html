What is AYUSH?

AYUSH stands for Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy, the traditional systems of medicine recognized and promoted by the Government of India. Each system brings its unique approach to health and wellness, rooted in natural remedies and holistic practices.

Ayurveda

An ancient system of medicine focusing on balance and harmony within the body through diet, herbal treatments, and lifestyle adjustments.

Yoga & Naturopathy

Emphasizes self-healing through natural remedies, physical postures, and holistic health practices.

Siddha

A South Indian tradition of medicine using herbs, minerals, and other natural substances to restore balance and health.

Homeopathy

A system of medicine based on the principle of "like cures like," using highly diluted substances to stimulate the body’s healing processes.
